The dominant theme is convergence: ECHA is simultaneously targeting legacy halogenated flame retardants (DBDPE), common industrial solvents (n-hexane), and fluorinated bisphenols that intersect directly with PFAS regulatory pressure. Activity is concentrated in a short burst rather than spread across the period, suggesting ECHA batch-processing additions ahead of anticipated Authorization List updates.
