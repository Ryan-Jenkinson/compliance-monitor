CA's construction market is among the largest in the US.

IL
Illinois has 2 bills in committee including the deforestation procurement bill that moved on 2026-03-13, directly relevant to wood-frame window and door manufacturers. IL is one of the top 5 US construction markets and a major windows/doors distribution hub.

If the company uses non-certified wood in product lines sold to IL government or public construction projects, initiate FSC or equivalent certification process for wood suppliers now — certification lead times are 6–18 months.

NY is the second-largest public construction procurement market in the US and frequently enacts supply chain transparency requirements that other states adopt.
→ Map all NY state and MTA/public authority contracts in the company's sales pipeline. Prepare supply chain transparency disclosures covering aluminum, glass, hardware, and sealant sourcing. Engage NY procurement counsel to understand certification format requirements before the bill reaches enactment.
